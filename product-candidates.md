## NOTES FOR GELATO PREP

- Web-ready files are all 1500×1000px — borderline for A2/A1 prints. For large format, check if original RAW/NEF/TIFF exists in the parent folder.
- SHERO patch (8500×5000) and TIFFs are the only confirmed high-res originals — but currently CLOUD only.
- Tufting photographs are photos *of* textile art, not the pattern itself — they work as art prints but the image includes the room/background.
- 1500×1000 = ~50×33cm at 72dpi, ~13×8cm at 300dpi. Gelato requires 300dpi min. Original files needed for anything above postcard size.
- Next step: identify which items have high-res originals (NEF/TIFF/DNG in parent folders), and sync those from Dropbox cloud.
